SageMath, Inc. Pricing (April 3, 2015)

« Other Policies

SageMathCloud charges for usage on a monthly basis, using the following pricing. Listed prices are in US dollars.

When charging in local currency, SageMath, Inc. will convert the prices listed into local currency using the conversion rates published by leading financial institutions.

Plan types

Warning: The following prices are very preliminary and no product is currently available for purchase.

SMC grows with you! The following upgrades allow you to increase the storage space for your files, the computational power and memory for your calculations, give your project network access, and let remove restrictions on non-commercial/non-academic use.
By upgrading one of your projects to a paid monthly plan, your files are copied to faster computers that are reserved only for pay projects, and you can start working right away. If you cancel your plan8, your files move back to slower free-1 machines. In any case, you do not lose your data and we regularly snapshot and backup your files across multiple data centers.

  1. Commercial use: free-1 projects may only be used for academic and non-commercial use.
  2. Network access: Access from your project to the outside Internet, so you can pull files from external data sources easily.
  3. Idle timeout: Your project will stop running if no files are edited for this amount of time. The project could also stop if the underlying machine crashes due to excessive use, or the machine is rebooted for maintenance.
  4. Disk space: The total mount of disk space that your project may use. This does not count snapshots and other backups.
  5. Speed: The Opteron processors are significantly slower than the Xeon's (often a factor of 2). Shared processors may be shared with other users, so you are not guaranteed to always get the full speed.
  6. Memory: The total amount of memory (RAM) that your project may use. Shared memory is shared with other users, so you are not guaranteed to always get the full amount of memory.
  7. Backups: Browsable snapshots of all files in your project are saved periodically.
  8. Price per month: Prices are not prorated. If you sign up you will be charged for a complete month.
Further Notes:
  • All free-1 projects run on a single crowded pool of computers, whereas the share projects run on their own completely separate pool of much less crowded computers.
